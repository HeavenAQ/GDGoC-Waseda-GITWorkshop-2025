About Our Circle

We are a student developer community at Waseda University where passionate students learn, build, and innovate together. Whether you're a complete beginner or experienced developer, GDG at Waseda is your space to grow.

Full-Stack Development

Learn front-end and back-end technologies through hands-on practice. We explore modern web development tools.

Collaborative Projects

Build real-world applications in teams while learning version control, agile development, and professional collaboration skills.

Regular Events

Weekly workshops, hack nights, guest speakers from industry, and hackathons to put your skills to the test.
